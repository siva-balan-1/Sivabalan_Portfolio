Projects

I has spearheaded a diverse range of innovative projects, showcasing expertise in electronics, programming, and problem-solving. Notable projects include a Smart Road Sign Detection and Vehicle Control System using CNN and Raspberry Pi, aimed at enhancing road safety, and Synthetic Medical Image Segmentation and Edge Detection, leveraging image processing techniques for medical diagnostics. He has also developed Lives Saving with Intelligence Li-Fi, exploring cutting-edge communication technology, and Leaf Disease Detection, applying image processing to agriculture. Additionally, two of his projects have been published as patents, reflecting their novelty and impact. These initiatives highlight his ability to address real-world challenges through technical innovation and interdisciplinary collaboration.
